# ✨ NEW FEATURE ADDED - Job-Based AI Summary Generation

## 🎉 What's New?

I've updated the **Professional Summary** section with a smart AI generator that creates customized summaries based on:
- ✅ **Target Job Role** (15 different roles to choose from)
- ✅ **Years of Experience** (from Fresher to 10+ years)
- ✅ **Your Skills** (automatically uses skills you've added)

---

## 🚀 How to See the Changes:

### **Step 1: Refresh Your Browser**

Since the frontend is already running, just:
1. Go to your browser (http://localhost:5173)
2. Press **Ctrl + Shift + R** (hard refresh)
3. Or press **F5** to refresh

The changes will appear immediately!

## 🎯 How to Use the New Feature:

### **Step 1: Fill Basic Info**
- Enter your **Name** (required)
- Add some **Skills** (optional but recommended)

### **Step 2: Customize AI Settings**
In the **Professional Summary** section, you'll now see a blue box with:

1. **Target Job Role** dropdown:
   - Software Developer
   - Frontend Developer
   - Backend Developer
   - Full Stack Developer
   - Data Scientist
   - Data Analyst
   - DevOps Engineer
   - Product Manager
   - UI/UX Designer
   - Mobile Developer
   - Machine Learning Engineer
   - Cybersecurity Analyst
   - Business Analyst
   - QA Engineer
   - Cloud Engineer

2. **Years of Experience** dropdown:
   - Fresher / Student
   - 1 Year
   - 2 Years
   - 3 Years
   - 4 Years
   - 5+ Years
   - 8+ Years
   - 10+ Years

### **Step 3: Generate AI Summary**
1. Select your **target job role** (e.g., "Frontend Developer")
2. Select your **experience level** (e.g., "2 Years")
3. Click **"AI Generate"** button
4. Watch as AI creates a customized professional summary!
