## 🎯 New Scoring System (100 Points Total):

### **1. Contact Information (10 points)**
- Name: 3 points
- Valid Email: 3 points
- Phone: 2 points
- Location: 2 points

### **2. Professional Summary (15 points)**
- Optimal length (150-300 characters): 15 points
- Good length (100-150): 12 points
- Short (50-100): 8 points
- Power words bonus: +2 points
- Checks for: "experienced", "skilled", "results-driven", etc.

### **3. Skills Section (20 points)**
- 10+ skills: 20 points (Excellent)
- 8-9 skills: 18 points (Very Good)
- 6-7 skills: 15 points (Good)
- 4-5 skills: 10 points (Fair)
- 1-3 skills: 5 points (Poor)

### **4. Work Experience (30 points)**
- Number of experiences: 10 points
  - 3+ experiences: 10 points
  - 2 experiences: 8 points
  - 1 experience: 6 points
- Description quality: 8 points
  - Detailed (100+ chars): 8 points
  - Moderate (50-100): 5 points
  - Brief (<50): 3 points
- Action verbs: 6 points
  - Uses strong verbs: 6 points
- Quantifiable metrics: 6 points
  - Has numbers/percentages: 6 points

### **5. Education (10 points)**
- Degree: 3 points per entry
- Institution: 2 points per entry
- Year: 1 point per entry
- Maximum: 10 points

### **6. Projects (5 points)**
- 2+ projects: 5 points
- 1 project: 3 points

### **7. Keyword Optimization (10 points)**
- 15+ keywords: 10 points (Excellent)
- 10-14 keywords: 7 points (Good)
- 5-9 keywords: 4 points (Fair)
- <5 keywords: 2 points (Poor)
