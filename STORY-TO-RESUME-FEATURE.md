# ✨ NEW FEATURE - Story-to-Resume Converter

## 🎉 What's New?

I've added a **Story-to-Resume Converter** that lets users write their background in plain text, and AI automatically converts it into a structured resume!

---

## 🎯 How It Works:

### **User writes a story like:**
```
Hi, I'm John Doe from San Francisco. I graduated from MIT with a 
Computer Science degree in 2020. I've been working as a Software 
Developer at Google for 2 years, where I developed web applications 
using React and Node.js. I also built a mobile app that got 10,000 
downloads. My skills include JavaScript, Python, React, AWS, and Docker. 
I'm passionate about creating user-friendly applications.
```

### **AI extracts and structures:**
- ✅ Name: John Doe
- ✅ Location: San Francisco
- ✅ Education: Computer Science degree from MIT (2020)
- ✅ Experience: Software Developer at Google (2 years)
- ✅ Skills: JavaScript, Python, React, AWS, Docker
- ✅ Projects: Mobile app with 10,000 downloads
- ✅ Professional Summary: Auto-generated

---

## 🚀 How to Use:

### **Step 1: Refresh Browser**
```
Press: Ctrl + Shift + R
```

### **Step 2: Click "Story-to-Resume" Button**
- Look at the top of the page
- You'll see a new **purple button** that says "Story-to-Resume"
- Click it!

### **Step 3: Write Your Story**
A modal will open with:
- Instructions on what to write
- Example text
- Large text area for your story

Write about:
- Your name and contact info
- Education background
- Work experience
- Skills and technologies
- Projects you've built
- Achievements

### **Step 4: Click "Convert to Resume"**
- AI processes your text
- Extracts structured information
- Shows you a preview

### **Step 5: Review and Apply**
- See what AI extracted
- Click "Apply to Resume" to add it to your resume
- Or click "Try Again" to rewrite

---

## 💡 What AI Extracts:

### **1. Personal Information**
- Name (from "I'm", "My name is")
- Email (email@example.com)
- Phone ((555) 123-4567)
- Location (city, state)

### **2. Education**
- Degree type (Bachelor, Master, PhD)
- Field of study
- University/College name
- Graduation year

### **3. Work Experience**
- Job titles (Developer, Engineer, Manager, etc.)
- Company names
- Duration (years)
- Job descriptions

### **4. Skills**
- Technical skills (JavaScript, Python, React, etc.)
- Tools (Git, Docker, AWS, etc.)
- Soft skills (Leadership, Communication, etc.)

### **5. Projects**
- Project names
- Technologies used
- Project descriptions

### **6. Professional Summary**
- Auto-generated from your story
- Highlights key points

## 🎯 Tips for Best Results:

### **Include These Keywords:**
- **Name:** "I'm", "My name is", "I am"
- **Education:** "graduated", "degree", "university", "studied"
- **Experience:** "worked", "developer", "engineer", "manager"
- **Skills:** Mention specific technologies
- **Projects:** "built", "created", "developed"
- **Achievements:** Include numbers and metrics

### **Be Specific:**
- ✅ "Software Developer at Google for 2 years"
- ❌ "Worked at a tech company"

- ✅ "Built a mobile app with 10,000 downloads"
- ❌ "Made an app"

- ✅ "Bachelor's in Computer Science from MIT, 2020"
- ❌ "Went to college"
